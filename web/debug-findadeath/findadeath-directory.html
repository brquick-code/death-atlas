Find a Death Directory

Welcome to the Find a Death Directory, a sprawling and alphabetized archive of celebrity deaths that’s equal parts fascinating and macabre. From Hollywood legends to pop culture icons, this list dives into the lives and final moments of the famous and infamous and it’s not just about how they died, but how they lived, what they left behind, and the strange, sometimes scandalous stories that followed them to the grave. If you’ve ever wondered where the stars stopped shining then this is your rabbit hole.
